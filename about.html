Artist bio

Abynx is an independent artist blending alt-pop songwriting, ambient score work, and visual storytelling. Her projects move between studio releases, live sessions, stage direction, and collaborative audiovisual concepts.

  • Based in New Delhi with remote collaborations across Europe and Southeast Asia.
  • Works across singles, film cues, visual campaigns, and live arrangements.
  • Available for booking, soundtrack work, and creative direction projects.
